About Me

Hello! My name is Anabelle Ahteck. I'm a senior at High Technology High School in Lincroft, New Jersey, and I like making things!

I have experience in research with wearable sensors and e-textiles for health monitoring and communication (check out the Research tab!).

I have some experience with circuit design and coding through Arduinos (3 years) and coding using Python (3 years), HTML(3 years), CSS(3 years), JSS (1 year), and Java (to the AP level).

I've been 3D modeling and printing for 4 years (check out the Projects tab!) using Fusion360. I also was an Autodesk Certified Associate in CAD for Mechanical Design (2023-2025).
