Unbreakable Faith (ORV)


Model Description

One of my favorite webnovels of all time: Omniscient Reader's Viewpoint. I read this webnovel throughout the pandemic, and after 551 chapters of learning and growing alongside the protagonist, Kim Dokja, this story holds its own place in my heart.

Of course, I wanted to pay homage to this story that stuck with me throughout the pandemic and all my years of high school. I 3D modeled Kim Dokja's sword, Unbreakable Faith.

This project was made primarily using extrusions, chamfers, and lofts.

3D Printer Settings

Maximum length: 44.625in
Maximum width: 12.125in
Maximum Thickness: 1.9in

Here were my 3D printer settings:
3D printer: Creality Ender 3 Pro
Slicer: Ultimaker Cura
Filament type: PLA
Layer Height: .2mm
Wall/Top/Bottom Thickness: .8mm
Infill: 20%
Printing Temp: 200 degrees C
Base Plate Temp: 60 degrees C
