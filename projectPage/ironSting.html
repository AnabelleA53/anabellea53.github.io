Iron Sting (From Genshin Impact)


Model Description

The Iron Sting is a craftable weapon from the game Genshin Impact. When I first learned 3D modeling, I wanted to make something cool, but knew I didn't have much of the knowledge to make something extremely sophisticated. The Iron Sting may look a little complicated design-wise, but it is very geometrically straight-forward, so I used it as my first project attempt.

3D Printer Settings

Maximum length: ~100cm
Maximum width: ~25cm
Has room to fit a 70cm long 1cm diameter wooden down as a skeleton. Intended to fit on a 220x220x220mm base plate.

Here were my 3D printer settings:
3D printer: Creality Ender 3 Pro
Slicer: Ultimaker Cura
Filament type: PLA
Layer Height: .2mm
Wall/Top/Bottom Thickness: .8mm
Infill: 20%
Printing Temp: 200 degrees C
Base Plate Temp: 60 degrees C
