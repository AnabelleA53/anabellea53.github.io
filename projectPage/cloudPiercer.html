Cloud-Piercer (From Honkai: Star Rail)


Model Description

Cloud-Piercer was probably my first large-project model. After three days of winter break and using all I knew about 3D modeling at the time, Cloud-Piercer was what truly sparked my love for 3D modeling.

Cloud-Piercer belongs to a character named Dan Heng, and this model serves as a functional cosplay prop.

3D Printer Settings

Maximum length: ~181.7cm
Maximum width: ~10cm
Has room to fit a 150cm long 2cm diameter wooden down as a skeleton. Intended to fit on a 220x220x220mm base plate.

Here were my 3D printer settings:
3D printer: Creality Ender 3 Pro
Slicer: Ultimaker Cura
Filament type: PLA
Layer Height: .2mm
Wall/Top/Bottom Thickness: .8mm
Infill: 20%
Printing Temp: 200 degrees C
Base Plate Temp: 60 degrees C
